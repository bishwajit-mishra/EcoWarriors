About Our Platform

Eco-Warriors is a revolutionary platform that transforms environmental learning from theoretical knowledge into an engaging, practical, and fun experience. We use gamification to motivate students to adopt sustainable habits and understand the direct impact of their actions.

Our platform aligns with India's SDG goals and NEP 2020's emphasis on experiential learning. By gamifying education, we increase student engagement and retention, empowering the next generation to become proactive environmental stewards.

Key Features

Interactive Lessons

Short, engaging modules on topics like climate change, waste management, and biodiversity.

Gamified Challenges

Earn eco-points, badges, and rewards for completing real-world tasks like tree-planting or waste segregation.

Quizzes and Games

Test your knowledge with fun and quick quizzes on all environmental topics.
